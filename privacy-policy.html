Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 October 2025

Kingzdata (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides a mobile app that lets users buy mobile data and airtime (“the App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

  • Account & Contact Info (if you create or provide it): name, email, phone number.
  • Transaction Details: mobile network, recipient number, bundle/amount, timestamps, and status.
  • Payment Information: processed securely by our payment processor; we do not store card numbers. We receive payment confirmations and references.
  • Device & App Info: app version, device model/OS, and diagnostics/logs to improve reliability.

How We Use Information

  • Process purchases of data and airtime and deliver receipts/status updates.
  • Provide customer support and resolve disputes.
  • Prevent fraud, secure our services, and comply with legal obligations.
  • Improve app performance and user experience.

Sharing & Disclosure

We may share information with:

  • Mobile network/service vendors to fulfill top-ups.
  • Payment processors to handle payments (we do not store card details).
  • Service providers that host infrastructure, analytics, or support services under contracts that protect your data.
  • Law enforcement or regulators when required by law.

Payments

All payments are handled by our third-party payment processor. Your card details are transmitted directly to the processor over encrypted connections and are not stored on our servers. We receive payment confirmations (e.g., transaction reference) to complete your order.

Data Retention

We keep transaction records and related information for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with our legal/accounting obligations, and resolve disputes. Where retention is no longer necessary, we delete or anonymize data.

Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect information, including encryption in transit, access controls, and monitoring. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Choices & Rights

  • You can view your recent transactions in the App.
  • You can contact us to correct or delete personal information we hold about you, subject to legal obligations.
