How to request deletion

You can request deletion of your Kingzdata account and associated personal data using either method:

  1. Email (recommended): Click the button below or email kingssub1401@gmail.com with the subject “Account Deletion Request”. Please include the email/phone used in the app and your last successful order reference (if available).

Email deletion request →

Verification

To protect your account, we may ask you to verify your identity by replying from your registered email or confirming a code sent to your registered phone number.

What will be deleted

  • Account profile: name, email, phone number, hashed password.
  • Saved beneficiaries/contacts (if stored in your account).
  • In-app preferences tied to your account.

What may be retained

Certain records are retained where required for legal, regulatory, fraud prevention, or accounting purposes. Examples:

  • Transaction records (e.g., network, recipient phone, bundle/amount, timestamps, status, payment references).
  • Security & audit logs necessary to ensure service integrity.

When retention is no longer required, we delete or anonymize this information.

Data shared with processors

Payments are processed by third parties (e.g., PalmPay, OPay, and participating Payment Service Banks). We will process your deletion request for data we control and, where feasible, relay necessary requests to our processors. You can also contact your payment provider directly for their records.

Timeline

  • We acknowledge requests within 7 days.
  • Deletion of account data is completed within 30 days after verification, subject to lawful retention obligations (see above).

Orders in progress

If an order is in progress when you request deletion, we’ll complete or cancel/refund it (as applicable) before closing the account.

After deletion

  • You’ll lose access to your account and in-app history.
  • You can still request receipts for completed purchases retained under legal/accounting requirements.
